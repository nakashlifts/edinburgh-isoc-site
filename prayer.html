Prayer Spaces On & Near Campus

Wherever you are studying, there's somewhere to pray nearby.

🕌

Edinburgh Central Mosque

Potterrow — the main mosque beside central campus, with daily prayers and Jumu'ah. Just a short walk from George Square and the Main Library.

🚪

University Prayer Rooms

Multi-faith and dedicated prayer facilities are available across university sites including the Chaplaincy Centre (Bristo Square) and Main Library area. Check signage or ask ISoc for the nearest one.

🏛️

King's Buildings

Science and engineering students at KB have prayer facilities on-site — ask the committee for the current room.

🤝

Jumu'ah Prayer

Friday prayer is held at Edinburgh Central Mosque and, during term, often arranged closer to campus by ISoc. Times are shared weekly on Instagram.

Qibla Direction

Facing the Ka'bah from Edinburgh

From Edinburgh, the Qibla is approximately 119° — roughly Southeast (ESE). Use a compass or a Qibla app (such as Muslim Pro or the iOS/Android compass) to line up precisely before you pray.

In most campus prayer rooms the direction is already marked — look for an arrow or mihrab indicator on the wall.

Halal Food Near Campus

Plenty of halal options around the central campus area and beyond.

🍛

Nicolson Street & Square

A strip of halal takeaways, kebab houses and Middle Eastern spots minutes from central campus.

🍜

Newington & Southside

Curry houses, grills and grocery stores serving the student community south of the Meadows.

🥗

Around Potterrow

Cafes and eateries near the mosque and student union — ask ISoc for our up-to-date halal map.
