Who We Are

About Edinburgh ISoc

A society built on faith, brotherhood and sisterhood, and a shared journey through university life.

Our Story

A home for Muslim students in Edinburgh

The University of Edinburgh Islamic Society exists to support every Muslim student during their time at university — spiritually, socially, and academically. From the moment you arrive in this beautiful city, we want you to know you belong.

We run weekly halaqahs, regular socials, charity initiatives, and our flagship Islam Awareness Week — all while making sure students can pray, eat halal, and find friendship wherever they are on campus.

What we stand for

Our values shape everything we do as a society.

🧭

Faith First

Keeping Allah at the centre of student life, and helping each other hold onto our deen through busy terms.

🌐

Inclusive Ummah

Every background, every madhhab, every level of practice — all are welcome here, without judgement.

🤲

Service

Giving back to Edinburgh and the wider world through charity, volunteering, and good character.
