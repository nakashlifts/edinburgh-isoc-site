About Edinburgh ISoc

Two decades of brotherhood, sisterhood and faith at the University of Edinburgh.

Our story

A home away from home since 2001

Founded in 2001, the University of Edinburgh Islamic Society has spent more than two decades supporting Muslim students spiritually, socially and academically. From a small group of students praying together between lectures, we have grown into one of the most active and beloved societies on campus — a genuine family for students arriving from every corner of the world.

We are proud winners of the FOSIS Best Collaboration Event 2025, a reflection of our commitment to working hand-in-hand with other societies and communities. At the heart of everything is our weekly halaqah, held every Tuesday at 5pm at the Chaplaincy, Potterrow — a calm, welcoming circle of knowledge and reflection that anchors our week and our community.

What we stand for

Our values

Three principles guide everything we do as a society.

🕌

Faith First

Worship, knowledge and remembrance of Allah sit at the centre of our society. We help one another keep faith strong through the demands of student life.

🌍

Inclusive Ummah

Every background, culture and level of practice is welcome. We are one ummah — and whoever you are, there is a place for you with us.

🥲

Service

From charity fundraisers to community volunteering, we strive to give back to Edinburgh and beyond with generosity and sincerity.

Your committee

The team behind ISoc

A dedicated student committee runs the society each year. Roles are filled by election at our Annual General Meeting (AGM).

Leadership

President

Leads the society, sets the vision for the year, and represents ISoc to the University, EUSA and FOSIS.

Leadership

Vice-President & Secretary

Supports the President, coordinates the committee, and keeps our meetings, records and communications running smoothly.

Finance

Treasurer

Manages the society’s budget, ticketing and fundraising, ensuring every penny is spent transparently and wisely.

Welfare

Sisters’ Officer

Champions the sisters’ community, organises sisters’ socials and ensures every sister feels welcome and supported.

Welfare

Brothers’ Officer

Champions the brothers’ community, runs brothers’ socials and sports, and looks out for the wellbeing of the brothers.

Outreach

Events & Media

Plans our calendar of events and tells the ISoc story online — from Instagram to Islam Awareness Week.

Interested in joining the committee? Roles are elected at the AGM — get in touch to find out more.
