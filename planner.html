About the Planner

A planner made for Muslim students, by Muslim students

160 pages of warm, Islamically-grounded planning — designed to help you stay consistent in your worship while you smash your academic year. Printed as a premium A5 booklet you'll be proud to carry.

£5 – £10

Available at the Freshers' Fair and ISoc events.

Reserve your copy

What's inside

Eleven sections covering every part of your year.

📜

Opening & Identity

A welcome letter, a niyyah, and a “Who Am I?” page to set your goals for the year.

🕌

Islamic Essentials

Prayer guide, Qibla, wudu steps, key du'as, and the Islamic calendar for 2026–27.

🗓️

Monthly Planners

Nine months, each with a cover, calendar grid, and reflection page.

📅

Weekly Spreads

Timetable, prayer & Qur'an trackers, priorities and a rotating reminder every week.

🌙

Ramadan Planner

A full 30-day tracker, khatm goals, and Laylatul Qadr & Eid checklists.

📈

Development Trackers

Qur'an khatm, habits, reading list, skills, sadaqah log and istighfar counter.
