Inside the planner

What’s inside

Every section is crafted to nourish both your deen and your day-to-day student life.

🌟

Opening & Identity

A warm welcome, intentions for the year, and space to reflect on who you want to become.

📖

Islamic Essentials

Key du’as, reminders and references to keep the fundamentals of your deen close at hand.

🗓️

Monthly Planners

Month-at-a-glance spreads with the Hijri calendar alongside the Gregorian months.

🗒️

Weekly Spreads

Plan lectures, deadlines and worship side by side, with room for daily prayers and goals.

🌙

Ramadan Planner

A dedicated section to make the most of the blessed month — fasting, Qur’an and good deeds.

📈

Development Trackers

Prayer, Qur’an, habit and reflection trackers to help you grow consistently all year.

“The most beloved deeds to Allah are the most consistent, even if small.”

Bukhari & Muslim
