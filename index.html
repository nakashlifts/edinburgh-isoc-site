Why ISoc

More than a society — a family

Whatever brought you to Edinburgh, you belong here. Here is what being part of ISocEd means.

🤝

Community

Meet brothers and sisters from every background and course. Socials, dinners and study sessions where lifelong friendships begin.

🕌

Worship

Pray together on campus and at Edinburgh Central Mosque, join our weekly halaqah, and keep your salah strong through busy term-time.

🪴

Support

Far from home? Our committee and members are here for a chat, a brew, or guidance through university life — you are never on your own.

🍽️

Halal Life

From the best halal eats around campus to prayer spaces and Jumu’ah, we help you live your deen comfortably in Edinburgh.

📖

Knowledge

Halaqahs, guest speakers and Islam Awareness Week nourish the mind and heart — seeking knowledge as an act of worship.

🥲

Charity

Fundraisers, volunteering and community service let us give back together, embodying the generosity at the heart of our faith.
