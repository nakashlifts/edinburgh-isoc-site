Why ISoc

Everything you need, in one community

Whether you're a fresher finding your feet or a final-year student, ISoc is here for every part of your journey.

🤝

Community

Weekly halaqahs, brothers' and sisters' socials, and a network of Muslim students who quickly become family.

🕌

Worship

Jumu'ah, prayer spaces across campus, and group iftars throughout Ramadan — keep your deen strong while you study.

🎓

Support

Mentorship, academic advice, and a friendly face whenever uni life gets tough. You're never on your own here.

🍛

Halal Life

Halal food guides, charity drives, and trips — living a wholesome student life the Edinburgh way.

📖

Knowledge

Talks, Qur'an circles, and Islam Awareness Week — growing in both sacred and worldly knowledge.

💚

Charity

Fundraisers and volunteering that turn our community's energy into real good for those in need.
